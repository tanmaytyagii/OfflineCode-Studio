# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in OfflineCode Studio welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of experience, education, identity, disability, appearance, nationality, or background.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume mistakes are opportunities to improve documentation and tooling.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's source, logs, or personal paths without consent.
- Accept maintainer decisions while using documented appeal or discussion channels constructively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and sustained personal attacks are not accepted in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer security/contact address configured for the repository. Maintainers will review promptly, protect reporter privacy as far as possible, and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants based on impact and context. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.

This concise policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant. Before public launch, maintainers should publish a monitored contact address and may adopt the full Contributor Covenant 2.1 text.
